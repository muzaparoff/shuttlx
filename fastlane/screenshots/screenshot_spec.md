# ShuttlX App Store Screenshots Spec

## Required Sizes (App Store Connect)
- iPhone 6.9" (1320 x 2868) — iPhone 16 Pro Max
- iPhone 6.7" (1290 x 2796) — iPhone 15 Pro Max  
- iPhone 6.5" (1284 x 2778) — iPhone 14 Plus
- Apple Watch Ultra (410 x 502)
- Apple Watch Series 10 (416 x 496)

## Screenshot Plan (6 screens, each with marketing copy)

### Screen 1: Hero — Timer in Action
**Theme:** Synthwave (most visually striking)
**View:** Training view mid-workout with timer counting down, heart rate zone glowing
**Copy:** "Interval Training, Perfected"
**Sub:** "Custom workouts on iPhone & Apple Watch"

### Screen 2: Apple Watch Companion
**Theme:** Clean
**View:** Split screen — iPhone Live Activity on left, Watch training view on right
**Copy:** "Train From Your Wrist"
**Sub:** "Live heart rate, pace, distance — updated every second"

### Screen 3: Template Builder
**Theme:** Clean
**View:** Template editor with work/rest intervals, repeat count, sport picker
**Copy:** "Build Any Workout"
**Sub:** "Work, rest, warmup, cooldown — unlimited combinations"

### Screen 4: Analytics Dashboard
**Theme:** Clean
**View:** Analytics view with VO2max chart, fitness/fatigue scores, personal records
**Copy:** "See Your Progress"
**Sub:** "VO2max, training load, personal records, pace zones"

### Screen 5: 7 Themes
**Theme:** Grid showing all 7 themes side by side
**View:** Same screen (dashboard or timer) rendered in each theme
**Copy:** "7 Unique Themes"
**Sub:** "Synthwave, Mixtape, Arcade, and more — your style, your app"

### Screen 6: GPS Routes & Plans
**Theme:** Clean
**View:** Route map with elevation profile, or training plan progress
**Copy:** "Routes, Plans & More"
**Sub:** "GPS tracking, elevation profiles, built-in training plans"

## Design Guidelines
- Device frame: Modern iPhone 16 Pro / Apple Watch Ultra frame
- Background: Gradient matching the theme shown
- Marketing text: White or light, large bold sans-serif, positioned above device
- Subtitle: Smaller, lighter weight, below main copy
- No cluttered UI — show ONE clear feature per screenshot
- Screenshots should tell a story left-to-right: Train → Watch → Build → Track → Style → Explore
