# ShuttlX Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 1, 2026**

## Introduction

ShuttlX ("the App") is an interval training app for iOS and Apple Watch. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Health & Fitness Data
The App accesses Apple HealthKit to read and write:
- **Heart rate** during workouts
- **Active energy burned** (calories)
- **Distance** walked or run
- **Workout sessions** you complete

This data is used solely within the App to track your training progress and display workout metrics. We never sell, share, or transmit your health data to third parties.

### Sign in with Apple
If you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive:
- A unique, anonymous user identifier
- Your name (if you choose to share it)
- Your email (if you choose to share it)

This information is used only to identify your iCloud sync account. We do not use your email for marketing.

### iCloud Sync
When signed in, your workout sessions and custom workout templates are synced to your **private iCloud database** using Apple's CloudKit. This data:
- Is stored in your personal iCloud account
- Is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Can be deleted at any time by signing out or deleting the app

### Local-Only Data
If you choose not to sign in, all data is stored locally on your device and Apple Watch via a shared App Group container. No data leaves your device.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect analytics or usage data
- We do not use third-party SDKs or trackers
- We do not display advertisements
- We do not share data with third parties
- We have no server infrastructure; all sync uses Apple's iCloud

## Your Rights

### Data Deletion
You can delete all your data at any time:
1. **Local data**: Go to Settings > Data Management > Clear All Training Sessions
2. **iCloud data**: Sign out of the app, then delete the app. Your iCloud data will be removed.

### Data Portability
All your data is stored in standard formats accessible through Apple Health and iCloud.

## Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
