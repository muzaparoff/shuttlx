ShuttlX Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Overview

ShuttlX ("we", "our", "the app") is an interval training application for iOS and Apple Watch. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data ShuttlX collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

ShuttlX collects and processes the following data, all of which remains on your device or within your personal iCloud account:

Health & Fitness Data (via Apple HealthKit)

  • Heart Rate - Read during workouts to monitor exertion
  • Active Energy Burned - Read and written to track calories during training
  • Distance (Walking/Running) - Read and written to track workout distance
  • Workout Sessions - Written to save completed training sessions to Apple Health

App Data (stored locally)

  • Training Programs - Custom interval training programs you create
  • Training Sessions - History of completed workouts
  • App Preferences - Theme, sync interval, and other settings

How We Use Your Data

  • Health data is used solely to provide real-time workout tracking and training history within the app
  • Training programs and sessions are synced between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework
  • Data is stored locally on your device using App Groups shared storage
  • All data processing happens on-device

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal or health data to any third parties.

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are used
  • No advertising networks are integrated
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • Health data is never used for advertising or marketing purposes

Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Health data is protected by Apple's HealthKit security framework
  • Synced data between iPhone and Apple Watch uses Apple's encrypted WatchConnectivity protocol
  • No cloud servers or third-party databases are used

Your Rights

  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > ShuttlX
  • You can delete all training history from within the app (Settings > Clear All Training Sessions)
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

Children's Privacy

ShuttlX does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is a general fitness tool intended for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
